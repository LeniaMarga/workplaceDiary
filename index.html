About

The USB – Urban Sciences Building is a highly monitoring working environment – a ‘smart’ building’ - with approximately 15.000 data entry points and a public API. But what do these data mean for the building occupants’ wellbeing?

Purpose of this diary is to collect information from the building occupants’ reflecting their daily experience in the workplace.
